Eastwood Moonsalt

product description

The unmistakeable moon shaped Kawai Moonsault electric guitar was first introduced in 1982. Production continued until the 1990s. The moon theme ran up the fretboard with phase-of-the-moon position markers. A rare clear plexiglass Moonsault was also available, called The Crystal Moon. Hard to get your hands on any original these days for under $3-4,000, most of those originals had active electronics with phase switches, etc.
